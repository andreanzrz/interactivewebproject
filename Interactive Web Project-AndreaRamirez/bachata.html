Bachata is known for its specific guitar sound that comes from the Dominican Republic. According to iasorecords.com, Bachata was first associated with poverty, prostitution, and poverty. This association was taken so seriously that only one national radio would play it. Later on, these associations would transform the Bachata music style to a romantic style. As Bachata is today, it is at it's peak of popularity. It's listened throughout Latin America and iasorecords claims it to be the most popular Latin genre in New York City.

Now onto artists that I think would be great introductions to Bachata that I would highly recommend! I think the easiest transition to Bachata would be to start off with Prince Royce, whose real name is Geoffrey Royce Rojas, and was born in The Bronx, New York. Prince Royce made his debut album in 2010. The website allmusic.com states that Prince Royce's debut album was well received by Bachata fans and that the twenty-year old was favorably compared to Aventura and other urban-oriented artists from New York. Prince Royce later released his next album in 2012 called "Phase II", which reached platinum status and peaked at number 16 on the Billboard 200. He continued to release albums in 2013 and 2014, called "Soy el Mismo" and "Double Vision", respectively. The album "Double Vision" was sung primarily in English, a first for Prince Royce. He also later released his newest album "Five" in 2017.

The next artist that I will talk about is Romeo Santos. His full name is Anthony 'Romeo' Santos, and he was also born in The Bronx, New York. When Romeo Santos was just thirteen, he formed the group called Los Teenagers, which was very popular in the New York City area. In 1999, Romeo Santos signed a record deal with the label Premium Latin, and thoughtco.com states that the band then changed their name to Grupo Aventura. That same year that they signed was the year they released their first album "Generation Next", which was very well received. The next year, in 2002, the band released their second album called "We Broke The Rules". This album included the hit song "Obsesión', and it transformed Aventura into the most popular Latin boy band at the time. After many years of being in the group, Romeo Santos decided to leave the group and pursue a solo career in 2011. He proceeded to continue his success solo, by selling out three consecutive nights at Madison Square Garden in 2012, and selling out a double show at Yankee Stadium. His album "Formula Vol. 1" that was released in 2011 became the one of the most popular Latin music albums of that same year including the following year (2012). His second album "Formula Vol. 2", which was released in 2014, became the best-selling album of that year. His newest single is "Héroe Favorito", which was released in early 2017.
